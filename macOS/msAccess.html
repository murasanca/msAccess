To Open for the First Time:

P.S. It is recommended to keep the msAccess folder on Desktop.

  • Open Terminal.
  • Paste to Terminal:
    • nohup /Users/$(whoami)/Desktop/msAccess/msAccess -silent -enable-doh -dns-addr https://dns.adguard.com/dns-query -window-size 9 &>/dev/null & disown
  • Press enter.
  • Press “Done”.
  • Press to “System Settings > Privacy & Security > Security > Allow Anyway“.
  • Continue with the “To Open:” steps.


To Open:

  • Open Terminal.
  • Paste to Terminal:
    • nohup /Users/$(whoami)/Desktop/msAccess/msAccess -silent -enable-doh -dns-addr https://dns.adguard.com/dns-query -window-size 9 &>/dev/null & disown
  • Press enter.
  • Press “Open Anyway”.
  • Close Terminal.

P.S If you restart your computer, you will not have internet access because your local proxy settings will be active.


Recommended solution with msAccess:

  • Follow from the "To Open:" heading.

Solution without msAccess:

  • Go to “System Settings > Wi-Fi > Details… > Proxies”.
  • Close “Web proxy (HTTP)” and “Secure web proxy (HTTPS)”.
